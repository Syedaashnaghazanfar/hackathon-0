How Gemini handles data and works with other services

Gemini uses info from your device to help you

Gemini uses this info to provide more customised and context-aware help.  
For example, Gemini accesses certain system permissions and data, like call  
and message logs, and your contacts (to help you keep in touch by sending  
messages or calling). Gemini can also read information including screen  
content, page context and links, and off-screen context (to help you act on  
it).


Gemini works with other services

Gemini can respond with real-time info from other services, like Google  
Maps and Flights. You can also choose to connect services like Google  
Workspace and YouTube Music. If Gemini finds a connected app that can help,  
it will send information from your conversation and other relevant  
information to that connected app to help it generate a better response.

Things to know when speaking to Gemini

your input like a normal activation.
